1. Start the program
2. Set the starting position of the customer at their current location 
3. Ask the customer where they would like to go
4. Tell the customer which way to begin walking, either:
   - north
   - south 
   - east
   - west
5. While they are heading the correct way but have have not arrived:
     - Tell the customer to keep walking straight
   If they go the wrong way:
     - Tell the customer to move more north
     - Or tell the customer to move more south
     - Or tell the customer to move more east
     - Or tell the customer to move more west
6. When they reach the correct location say "You have arrived"
7. End the program
